Architecture

What changes when AI lives at the edge

The CDN moved static files closer to users. ICON moves meaning closer to users. Content is published once, stored permanently on IPFS. At every request, an edge agent reads context and composes the version of your content most likely to resonate — before the response leaves the server.

Four principles
AI on the Edge
Inference happens at delivery

Not a cloud API call after the page loads. Not a round-trip to a central AI server. Inference runs at the edge node closest to your visitor — so content is composed before the response leaves. This is what makes per-request personalization latency-free at any scale.

Hyper-Personalization
One piece of content. A billion variations.

You write your ideas once. ICON composes the version that fits each visitor's context — their intent, their background, their stage. Not two variants. Not five. A continuous composition space, constrained by your content, shaped by context.

Trustless Verification
Don't trust ICON. Trust the math.

Every piece of content is cryptographically signed via the PAIRL protocol at publish time. Visitors don't need to trust that what they're reading is authentic — they can verify it independently. No vendor assurances. The signature is the proof. Always.

Immutable by Design
Published means permanent

Content is stored on IPFS with a content address (CID). The CID is a hash of the content itself — change a word, the CID changes. What you published is what was read, forever. No silent edits. No retroactive rewrites. No drift between what you claimed and what visitors saw.

How it works

Three operations.
Zero infrastructure.

01

Write

Create content in your editor, your workflow. Articles, landing pages, docs — no new format, no migration. ICON works with what you already have.

02

Publish to IPFS

One command. ICON pins your content and returns a permanent CID. Immutable, decentralized, ready for composition. No database. No server to manage.

03

ICON composes

Every request triggers an edge agent. Reads context, retrieves content by CID, composes the right version. Signed, served, traceable. In under 100ms.
