What changes when AI lives at the edge

The CDN moved static files closer to users. ICON moves meaning closer to users. Your content is published once, stored permanently. Then, at every request, an edge agent reads context — device, referrer, behavior signals — and composes the version of your content most likely to resonate. Before the response leaves the server.

Built different at every layer

AI on the Edge

Inference happens at delivery

Not a cloud API call after the page loads. Not a round-trip to a central AI server. The inference runs at the edge node closest to your visitor — so content is composed before the response leaves. This is what makes per-request personalization latency-free at scale.

Hyper-Personalization

One piece of content. A billion variations.

You write your ideas once. ICON composes a version of them that fits each visitor's context — their intent, their background, their stage in the funnel. Not two variants. Not five. A continuous composition space, constrained by your content, shaped by context.

Trustless Verification

Don't trust ICON. Trust the math.

Every piece of content is cryptographically signed at publish time via the PAIRL protocol. Your visitors don't need to trust that what they're reading is authentic — they can verify it independently, without asking anyone. The signature is the proof. Always.

Immutable by Design

Published means permanent

Content is stored on IPFS with a content address (CID). The CID is a hash of the content itself — change a word, the CID changes. What you published is what was read, forever. No silent edits. No retroactive rewrites. No drift between what you claimed and what visitors saw.

How it works

Three operations. Zero infrastructure to manage.

01

Write your content

Create articles, landing pages, docs — in your editor, in your workflow. No new format, no migration. ICON works with structured content you already have.

02

Publish to IPFS

One command. ICON pins your content to IPFS and returns a permanent content address. Your content is now immutable, decentralized, and ready for composition. No database. No server.

03

ICON composes at the edge

Every request triggers an edge agent. It reads context, retrieves your content by CID, and composes the version that fits this visitor. The result is signed, served, and traceable. In under 100ms.
